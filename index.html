FAQ

Will I need a credit merchant account from my bank?
No! Through our partner, Stripe, we are able to offer per swipe transactions with no minimum. Sign up right through our app.
Are the credit transactions secure?
Yes! However, you will want to make sure you are connected to a secure wireless hotspot of your own. 3G and 4G is relatively safe, but look out for any unmarked vans in the neighborhood.
